about

I am a digital journalist and a builder of web things. I am currently a data intern at the investigative team of The Palm Beach Post. Before that, I was a Google Journalism Fellow at Poynter where I reported on the media industry, highlighting innovation and technology in journalism.


A strong believer of the power of technology and a big fan of the term ‘new media,’ I believe in telling multimedia stories that are not limited to the 800-word format. After all, the digital space is an interactive one, and it is best when kept that way. I am a recent graduate of the Columbia Journalism School, where I graduated with honors.


When not exploring the world of data and interactive storytelling, I write about technology and social media at different online portals.


When there are brain waves that don’t fit in ‘different portals,’ they end up in my blog.
