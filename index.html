about

I am a student at the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism. A strong believer of the power of technology and a big fan of the term ‘new media,’ I believe in telling multimedia stories that are not limited to the 800-word format. After all, the digital space is an interactive one, and it is best when kept that way.


Most recently, I have started doing cool things in R, and even cooler things in JavaScript.


When not exploring the world of data and interactive storytelling, I write about technology and social media at different online portals.


When there are brain waves that don’t fit in ‘different portals,’ they end up here.
